what is pink goku

Pink Goku is typically associated with Goku Black, a major antagonist from the Dragon Ball Super series, specifically during the "Future Trunks Saga." Goku Black is an alternate version of Goku, but with significant differences in origin, character, and powers

Background of Goku Black:

Goku Black is actually Zamasu, a Supreme Kai apprentice from Universe 10, who stole Goku’s body through the power of the Super Dragon Balls. Zamasu’s motivation was to bring "justice" to the multiverse by eliminating mortals, whom he viewed as flawed. He swapped bodies with Goku and became "Goku Black," using Goku’s appearance and powers but with Zamasu’s twisted ideals.

Super Saiyan Rosé (Pink Form):

The "Pink Goku" refers to Super Saiyan Rosé, a unique transformation used by Goku Black. This form is similar to Goku’s Super Saiyan Blue transformation, but instead of blue hair, Goku Black’s hair turns pink or rosé. This form represents the combination of Zamasu’s godly Ki and Goku’s Saiyan powers. Super Saiyan Rosé allows Goku Black to harness immense strength, speed, and fighting abilities while giving him a divine, elegant appearance with his pinkish aura and hair.

Key Events in Goku Black's Storyline:

  • introduction

    Goku Black first appears in Future Trunks’ timeline, where he wreaks havoc on Earth, killing many people, including Future Bulma, and forcing Trunks to seek help from the past.

  • Fights with Goku:

    Goku Black battles Goku, Vegeta, and Future Trunks multiple times. His Super Saiyan Rosé form often proves to be more powerful than Goku’s Super Saiyan Blue.

  • Zamasu's Immortality:

    In the end, Goku Black teams up with a future version of Zamasu, who becomes immortal. Together, they form an unstoppable duo, bent on destroying all life in the multiverse.

  • Defeat:

    Eventually, Goku Black and Zamasu are defeated with the help of Goku, Vegeta, Trunks, and the Zeno (the Omni-King), who erases the entire timeline to stop the menace.

    • ending

    Goku Black and his pink Super Saiyan Rosé form left a significant impact on Dragon Ball Super fans, becoming one of the most memorable villains in the series.
